❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How many players can play?

Mission: Wrecked supports 2 to 6 players. Just enter each crew member's name on the setup screen before launching.

Does the app require an internet connection?

No — the game works fully offline once downloaded. No accounts, no logins, no Wi-Fi required.

How long does a game take?

A typical game with 4 players takes around 45–90 minutes depending on how often special spaces trigger events and how long mini-games run.

I purchased an expansion pack but it's not showing up.

Go to your device Settings → App Store → make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the purchase. Then open the app and tap Restore Purchases. If the issue persists, email us with your order number.

How do I reset the game mid-session?

Tap the ↩ icon in the top right corner of the game screen at any time to reset and start a new game.

The app crashed or something isn't working correctly.

Try closing and reopening the app first. If the issue continues, email us at the address above and describe what happened — we'll get it fixed in an update.

Who is this app intended for?

Mission: Wrecked is intended for responsible adults aged 21 and older. Please drink responsibly and never drink and drive.
