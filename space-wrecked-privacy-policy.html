Privacy Policy

App: Space Wrecked  |  Developer: Moore Devs  |  Last updated: March 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Space Wrecked ("the App") handles information when you use it. The short version: we do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

1. Information We Collect

Space Wrecked does not collect any personal information. The App operates entirely on your device. We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or account information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Payment information
  • Any other personal or sensitive data

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores a single item locally on your device: a flag indicating that you have confirmed you are 21 years of age or older. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. You can clear it at any time by deleting the App.

3. Third-Party Services

Space Wrecked does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data services. No SDKs that collect data are included in the App.

4. Children's Privacy

Space Wrecked is intended solely for adults aged 21 and older. The App includes an age verification gate and is rated 17+ on the App Store. We do not knowingly collect any information from minors. If you believe a minor has accessed the App, please delete it from their device.

5. In-App Purchases

Any purchases made within the App are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive, process, or store your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs those transactions and can be found at apple.com/legal/privacy.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We do not anticipate material changes given that no data is collected.
